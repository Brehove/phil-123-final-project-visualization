PHIL 123 Final Project

Build Like a Philosopher.

This capstone is not a stack of separate assignments. It is one long design journey where students research, define a meaningful problem, choose a pathway, build toward a public-facing artifact, and explain every major ethical choice they made along the way.

What should I build, and why?

Double Diamond, translated into course time

Modules 8-10 Discover

Deep research across three AI ethics zones.

Module 11 Define

Translate broad research into one clear, actionable problem.

Module 12 Develop

Compare concepts, weigh tradeoffs, and commit to one direction.

Module 13 Spec

Turn values into a detailed plan with stakeholders, risks, and scope.

Modules 14-15 Draft + Revise

Build, self-assess, gather peer feedback, and iterate.

Module 16 Deliver

Submit the final artifact and explain the process behind it.
